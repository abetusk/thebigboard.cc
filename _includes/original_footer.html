METHODOLOGY: Year-to-date worldwide COVID-19 deaths are from Johns Hopkins University. Worldwide deaths from other causes are from the Global Burden of Disease Study, using 2017 estimates prorated to the current point in 2020 (assuming linear growth over the course of the year).

This list is not intended to be exhaustive; I've chosen a sample that includes many familiar diseases and causes of death and omits many others. Also note that grouping has a large impact on the raw numbers; "cancer" encompasses a larger number of underlying conditions than "whooping cough".

Causes of death have been edited for length, ranging from the benign (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease ➞ COPD) to the potentially misleading (other exposure to mechanical forces ➞ crushing); both the data driving this page and the original data I exported from GBD are available on GitHub.
